IVE KILLED A BUNCH OF ENEMIES, BUT ALEX HASNT LEVELED UP YET?!

In Chapter Two (shortly after the Factory Hotel scenario), Alex meets a character who teaches him how to use the Mind Dungeon to level up. Once youve gained access to the Mind Dungeon you will be able to spend the EXP youve acquired in battle to level up.

HOW DO I RUN?

Square on PlayStation and Y on Nintendo Switch.

I FORGOT WHERE I WAS SUPPOSED TO GO AND IM LOST. WHERE DO I GO NEXT!?

In the Options Menu (F2 on PC), there is a hint option that tells you where to go next.

HOW DO I SORT ITEMS?

Open the menu and select Items. I on PC, Triangle on PlayStation, and X on Nintendo Switch.

HOW DO I SCROLL THROUGH THE INVENTORY SYSTEM FASTER?

You can scroll quickly through menus by holding the Run Button. j on PC, Square on PlayStation, and Y on Nintendo Switch,

WHERE CAN I FIND MT. TOWN KEYS?

KEY 1 can be found in the second room of the cave dungeon across the poison pathway. The second key can be found on the bottom floor of the dungeon in the middle of three chests.

ESSENTIA'S MIND DUNGEON: PATH OF DARKNESS

When the lights are off, the borders surrounding this room disappear, allowing you to walk through and wrap around to the other side of the puzzle. The goal is to retrieve the key, blocked by a gate. This gate can be unlocked by flipping the red switch. Alex will only be able to interact with switches and chests when candles are lit.
